================================================================================
  AUDIOBOOKS GENERATOR — App Description, Features & Keywords
================================================================================

APP NAME
  Audiobooks Generator

SHORT DESCRIPTION (1–2 sentences)
  Convert any text, PDF, DOCX, or TXT file into a downloadable MP3 audiobook
  in seconds. Powered by Google Text-to-Speech (gTTS), this tool lets you
  listen to documents, articles, and notes hands-free — no account required.

FULL DESCRIPTION
  The Audiobooks Generator is a browser-based tool that transforms written
  content into high-quality audio files you can download and listen to
  anywhere. Whether you're a student who wants to review lecture notes on
  the go, a professional catching up on reports during a commute, or anyone
  who finds listening easier than reading, this tool does the heavy lifting.

  Simply paste your text directly into the editor, or upload a supported
  document (PDF, Word DOCX, or plain TXT). The app extracts the text
  automatically, sends it to Google TTS for speech synthesis, and delivers
  a finished MP3 file you can download with one click.

  Jobs are processed in the background — you can close the tab and come back
  later, or watch the status update live without refreshing the page. Each
  job is listed in your history with download and delete controls.

HOW IT WORKS
  1. Paste text into the editor OR upload a PDF / DOCX / TXT file.
  2. Enter an optional title for your audiobook.
  3. Click "Generate Audiobook" — the job starts immediately in the background.
  4. The status updates live (no page refresh needed).
  5. When complete, click "⬇ MP3" to download your audio file.

FEATURES
  ● Text input — paste up to 50,000 characters directly
  ● File upload — supports PDF, Microsoft Word (.docx), and plain text (.txt)
  ● Automatic text extraction from PDF (PyMuPDF) and DOCX (python-docx)
  ● Background processing — jobs run server-side, non-blocking
  ● Live status polling — UI updates automatically every 2 seconds
  ● Direct server-side MP3 download (no browser blobs)
  ● Job history — see all past audiobooks with status badges
  ● Per-job delete — remove individual jobs and their files
  ● Character counter with visual warning near the limit
  ● Admin-configurable TTS language (any BCP-47 code: en, es, fr, de, ja…)
  ● Admin-configurable max character limit
  ● Mobile-responsive, dark-themed interface

SUPPORTED FILE TYPES
  ● .pdf  (text-based; scanned PDFs without OCR will produce limited output)
  ● .docx (Microsoft Word)
  ● .txt  (UTF-8 plain text)

LIMITATIONS
  ● Scanned/image-only PDFs are not supported (no OCR)
  ● gTTS requires an internet connection to the Google TTS API
  ● Audio quality depends on Google TTS; no SSML or voice selection

USE CASES
  ● Students listening to study notes or textbook chapters
  ● Accessibility: converting documents for visually impaired users
  ● Language learners hearing correct pronunciation of text
  ● Professionals listening to reports or emails during commutes
  ● Writers listening back to their own drafts to catch errors

TECH STACK
  Flask · gTTS · PyMuPDF · python-docx · SQLite · Python threading
